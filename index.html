Melbourne Facts

- Known as Australia’s cultural capital; recognised as the centre of sport, music, theatre, comedy, art, literature, film and television.

- Consistently ranked as the world’s most liveable cities by The Economist Intelligence Unit.

- Has the highest number of cafés and restaurants to people ratio than any other city in the world.

- Was originally named ‘Batmania’ after one of its founding fathers, John Batman.
